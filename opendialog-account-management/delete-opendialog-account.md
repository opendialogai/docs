# Delete OpenDialog account

In case you choose to discontinue OpenDialog, you can request deletion of your OpenDialog Cloud Edition account by following the instructions below.

1. Please contact [Support@OpenDialog.ai](<mailto:Support@OpenDialog.ai >) and incl. your instance ID within the email to request your account deletion.&#x20;
2. Once the email is received the OpenDialog customer support team will be in touch to confirm deletion and kickstart your account deletion.&#x20;
3. Once completed, you will be notified through email that your account has been deleted.

**Note:**  deleting your OpenDialog account will remove your entire account incl. all scenarios and will not be recoverable. Please make sure you have exported/transferred any content before requesting the deletion. \
